Shuffling Cards (Overhand Method)

This is a skill that takes a lot of practice but when mastered it will really impress people. Learning this skill will need patience and practice.
For this skill a ball is advised for use but if you do not have one you can easily use anything that is ball like, as long as it's not delicate and easily broken, i.e. unused toilet roll.

Tips: By watching the video you will be able to learn better.


Steps

1. Take out all the cards in a deck
2. Spread them out across the table in any order
3. Gather up all the cards
4. Hold The Deck in your left hand at a slight angle and 3 fingers on bottom card
5. Using your right hand put your thumb on the bottom of the deck and your index at the top left corner and 'slice' the deck slow dropping a number of cards into the slot and repeat
6. You Have successfully shuffled the deck using the overhand method

Difficulty: Medium

Duration: All depending on the individual, however with enough practice and determination, one can do this within the same day.
