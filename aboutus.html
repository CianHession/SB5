About Us

Our website was designed to free you from that boredom that occurs every while in our life. It is occurring more and more due to the current pandemic we face. Every one of us face the struggle to be active or motivate ourselves to do anything as there is not much to look forward to while in lockdown. Even without the pandemic we can struggle on the odd day to decide what to do with ourselves. Well this website was designed for this reason.


We also thought that it's only in the oddest of times that we need to use certain skills that only a rare amount of people has mastered. this can range from whistling with your fingers or even juggling. We have inputted many skills that we believe everyone can learn and it can all be done with the simple items that are in every household or just using your own body. We believe that learning these tasks will come in handy but most importantly free you from your boredom.



To use our website, it is simple. Just follow these easy steps:
1. Spin the wheel by clicking on the middle of the wheel.
2. Once the wheel chooses your task it will take u directly to the instructions. We have both text and video instructions
3. Learn that skill!
4. Once you have mastered the task, go back to the wheel and spin again.


We hope you learn from the Wheel of boredom and have fun doing it.
