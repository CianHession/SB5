The Plank

Easy and great exercise to do at home with absolutely 0 items required (apart from arms and legs I guess…)

Tips: Doing the plank is a fantastic core building exercise, but I wouldn’t push myself to over-do the body's limit i.e. When it starts to burn, stop when it's becoming very difficult and painful.


Steps

1. Lie on the floor, chest down
2. Get into the press-up position
3. Now put your elbows on the floor and keep straight
4. Keep your back straight and at a decent height above the ground
5. Do it for as-long as you can and you will feel it burning
6. You have done the plank and put in some good exercise for a while

Difficulty: Depending on your own strength and fitness

Duration: Depending on your own strength and fitness
