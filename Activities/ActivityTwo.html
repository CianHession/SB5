Learn how to make an Origami Paper Crane

Learning how to make a paper crane is very simple and will allow you to go on to make various other things with paper if you take a liking to it.
The only requirement is a piece of A4 Paper or square origami paper and an optional scissors.

Steps:

1. If you have an A4 Page take a corner and fold it into a square, the excess page can be cut off with scissors or you can fold that, crease it and tear it off.
2. You now should have a square that has been folded in half, you want to open it up and fold it again from the opposite corner.
3. Flip the page over and fold it in half, open it back up and fold it the other way as well.
4. Place the page in a diamond position, fold the sides into the bottom section and press the top down.
5. You should now have a small diamond with flaps, fold the flaps inwards, flip it over and fold inwards again and crease the top downwards.
6. Take the bottom flap and push it up, fold the creases on the side inside, flip it over and repeat the same thing.
7. Take the corner edges and fold it into the center for both sides and flip over and do the same.
8. Pinch the two flaps together and fold up and pinch together again.
9. Now fold the outsides over to get the wings, pull and pinch the middle outwards.
10. Push the middle of the head down and pinch it in.

Difficulty: Easy

Duration: 10 Minutes
