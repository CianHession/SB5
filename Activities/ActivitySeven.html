Learn how to do a Cartwheel

Cartwheeling may seem like an easy activity to achieve but it's more challenging than you may think! It's a staple in any gymnasts arsenal and is essential to becoming one!

Tips:This activity is best attempted in an open area or even outdoors! Before attempting to do a cartwheel make sure to warmup and stretch beforehand to avoid any injuries

Steps:

1. Begin in a lunge position facing forward, with your leading leg bent slightly
2. Raise your arms up by your ears
3. Begin to lower your hands to the ground, will bringing your back leg up - this should form a T position
4. Continue lowering until you can place your hand sideways on the ground, turning your body to do so
5. Push off your leading leg into the cartwheel motion
6. Follow through with the motion, while landing with your inital leading leg now behind

Difficulty: Medium

Duration: With enough practice, one can do a cartwheel on the first day!
