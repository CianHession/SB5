Learn how to Draw a Dog

Drawing might seem hard but is very easy to learn if you follow the right guide.
You will a page to draw on and a pencil.

Steps:

1. Draw the dog's eye with a dot on the page.
2. Draw a semi-circle line above the dog's eye.
3. Extend the front of the line to draw the dog's nose, draw a dot at the end of the line for his nose.
4. Under the nose draw a L shape inwards to complete the dog's mouth.
5. Under the inwards line continue down to draw the dog's neck.
6. At the other side of the top line, continue down and turn inwards to draw the dog's ear.
7. From the bottom of the ear draw down to complete the dog's back.
8. On the other side draw down to make the dog's chest and add two front legs at the bottom of the line.
9. Continue down the line and add the back legs and a tail.
10. You can finish by adding lines on the dog's paws and an ear behind the dog's head with dots on the mouth.

Difficulty: Easy

Duration: 3 Minutes
