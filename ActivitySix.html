Learn how to Whistle using your Fingers

What seems to come naturally to some, others may need more practice at this task. Its skill that is only needed in the rarest of times (such as getting somebodies attention from afar) and for that reason most people never learn to do it. Most people learn to whistle without using their fingers but doing it this way it is not as loud.

Tips: After a decent amount of practice you should begin to hear the whistle sound coming through. If you feel it is not working for you swap the 'A' using your index and middle finger with this next tip:

Steps:

1. Draw back your lips, down over your teeth. Make sure your lips are covering your teeth fully.
2. Then, form an 'A' using your index and middle fingers with both hands
3. Place your finger formation into your mouth
4. Point the tip of your tongue upwards, pushing your tongue back ever so slightly
5. Blow air through the gap in between your fingers. No air should be coming out the side of your mouth.

Difficulty: Medium

Duration: Practice makes perfect!
